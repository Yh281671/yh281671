Welcome, International Students! 🌏

A Home for Learners from Around the World

At Sanrio University, we believe in the power of global education. Each year, we welcome students from over 30 countries who bring diversity, ideas, and culture to our campus.

🌐 Application Process

Applying as an international student is simple. Submit your online application, upload academic documents, and attend a virtual interview. Our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

🛂 Student Visa & Accommodation

Once accepted, we will assist with student visa applications and offer a variety of housing options — from cozy dorms to Hello Kitty-themed homestays! 🏠

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Support & Integration

We provide language support classes, cultural orientation programmes, and one-on-one counselling to ensure you feel welcomed, confident, and connected.

🎉 Experience Life the Sanrio Way

Join student clubs, enjoy seasonal festivals, and take part in the Sanrio Global Buddy Programme — where every new student is paired with a friendly local peer.

✉️ Have questions? Contact our International Office at international@sanriouni.edu.
