Our Programmes

Empowering Bright Minds in Every Field 🎓

At Sanrio University, we offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes designed to equip students with knowledge, skills, and joy. Explore our faculties below!

⚖️ School of Law

Specialising in Family Law, Commercial Law, and International Law. Our law school focuses on justice with empathy and precision.

🎨 School of Arts & Design

From visual communication to digital storytelling, students explore creativity while learning industry-relevant skills.

📈 School of Business

Prepare for the global economy with courses in marketing, finance, entrepreneurship, and leadership development.

💻 School of Technology

Discover the future through our tech programmes in computer science, AI, UX/UI design, and cybersecurity.

🌍 School of International Studies

Train to become a global citizen with intercultural communication, diplomacy, and sustainable development courses.
